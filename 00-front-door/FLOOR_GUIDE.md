# Floor Guide / 樓層導覽

Welcome to the public entrance. / 歡迎來到公開入口。

This repo is arranged like a first-floor gallery: simple at the doorway, deeper as you walk in.
本倉庫的排列就像一樓畫廊：入口處簡單，越深入越深入。

---

## Lobby / 大廳

Start with the main idea / 從主要概念開始:

> AI systems need more than prompts. They need state, rules, handoff, and proof.
> AI 系統需要的不只是提示詞。它們需要狀態、規則、交接，以及證明。

---

## Gallery Wall / 展示牆

Five exhibits explain the work / 五個展示說明工作:

1. **Semantic State / 語義狀態** — What state is the AI in? / AI 處於什麼狀態？
2. **Semantic Integrity / 語義完整性** — Did the next AI inherit the right meaning? / 下一個 AI 是否繼承了正確的語義？
3. **Runtime Governance / 運行治理** — Should the system continue, halt, or verify? / 系統應繼續、停止還是驗證？
4. **Multi-LLM Coordination / 多模型協調** — How do different models hand off work? / 不同模型如何交接工作？
5. **Semantic Mapping / 語義映射** — How does a messy archive become navigable? / 雜亂的檔案如何變得可導航？

---

## Proof Counter / 證明台

The repo includes small public-safe artifacts. They are not full systems. They are inspection windows.
本倉庫包含小型公開安全的構件。它們不是完整系統。它們是檢視窗口。
