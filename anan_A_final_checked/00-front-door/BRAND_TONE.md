# Brand Tone

## Desired feel

- quiet confidence
- clear structure
- high density
- low hype
- serious but readable
- public-safe

## Avoid

- AGI manifesto tone
- excessive acronyms at the entrance
- military / war-room aesthetics
- flashy AI buzzwords
- claims that sound externally proven when they are internal

## Use

- state
- handoff
- boundary
- trace
- proof surface
- semantic map
- governance rule

## Do not use as headline language

- complete AGI governance stack
- universal semantic theory
- solved alignment
- production safety guarantee
