# Exhibit 04 — Multi-LLM Coordination

## Simple layer

When many AIs work together, the problem is not only intelligence.

The problem is coordination.

Different models may disagree, forget constraints, or misunderstand what was already decided.

## Technical layer

This line treats multi-model work as a stateful coordination problem.
